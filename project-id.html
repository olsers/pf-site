5ish years of Industrial Design

TL;DR

I started studying Industrial Design in 2012 and had a career in the field until around 2020. I wonder what happened then.... 🤔 In that time I designed a range of objects, from consumer electronics concepts, to high-end furniture, and even food packaging for some of the world's biggest airlines. This isn't a full ID portfolio — rather than write case studies for these older projects, I wanted to at least acknowledge my first career here, since it was a major part of shaping me as a designer today. So please enjoy this wall of images and reach out if you have any questions!
