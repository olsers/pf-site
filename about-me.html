About me

Work stuff

I’m a UX generalist with a particular passion for the small details. I’ve spent the last few years creating workflow-centric, web-based software for a variety of financial products. These workflows ranged from big data ETL (extract, transform, load), front-office dashboards, and everything inbetween. My UX career started after completing a Masters in Human-computer Interaction, prior to which I was an Industrial Designer working in London.
